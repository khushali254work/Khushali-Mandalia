Soda Can

For this project we were asked to choose one historic movement. After choosing 1 movement we were asked to do some visual reserach and use the elements of that period and designing the packaging accordingly. I chose the gothic period as I felt that I understood the elements of that period very well. I have created a soda can / energy drink packaging. I made 3 different designs for it showcasing various different elemnets from the gothic period.

Incense packaging

For me, this assignment was really interesting as I did not have much knowledge about this period and art works from this period. I explored a lot of different motives and elements while doing this project. I used colours that related to the period the most for example the power blue and gold. I also tried to keep the design simple yet related to the theme by adding floral illustrations in the background. I wanted to portray a more royale look to it and so chose to keep it as clean as straightforward and effortless as possible.

Coffee Cup

Personally, I learned a lot about the Neoclassical period and tried my best to inculcate the design elements that I found unique, into my packaging design. I did a lot of visual research in order to find something different and uncommon. Some of the common characteristics seen in all Neoclassical artworks include: symmetry, unemotional telling of events, simplicity of line, form, and colour, balance of straight lines & geometric shapes, use of science, mathematics etc. Thus I tried to incorporate these elements in my design too.
